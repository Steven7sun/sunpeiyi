ZipRecuriter is a leading online employment marketplace that actively connects millions of businesses and job seekers through a range of AI-driven platforms and experiences. With a long view of creating a future in which no one has to search for work, ZipRecuriter is interested in engaging in a process of innovation that calls for a co-design of these futures with the end user.In this project I designed a prototype of a future photo marketplace for ziprecruiter and explored future possibilities with my group colleagues
